Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Fill out the order form on our Order page, submit it, and WhatsApp will open automatically with your order details. Just send the message and we'll confirm.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We deliver throughout Nairobi. Free delivery applies to orders above KSH 10,000. For orders below that, delivery charges will be confirmed via WhatsApp.

Can I pick up from your location?

Absolutely! You can select "Pickup" when placing your order and collect from Dagoreti Slaughter House at your preferred time.

How long does delivery take?

Same-day delivery is available for orders placed in the morning. Exact timing will be confirmed via WhatsApp based on your preferred time.

Is the meat fresh?

Yes! All meat is prepared fresh when you place your order, never pre-packaged. We maintain strict hygiene standards at Dagoreti Slaughter House.

Can I modify my order after submitting?

Once you send the message on WhatsApp, you can communicate directly with us to make any changes before we start preparation.

Do you offer bulk orders?

Yes! For bulk or wholesale inquiries, please contact us on WhatsApp at 0701698883 to discuss pricing and availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

Payment methods will be discussed and confirmed via WhatsApp. We work with customers to find convenient payment solutions.
