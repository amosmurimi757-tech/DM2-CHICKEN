About Fresh Meat Daily

Your trusted local source for fresh, high-quality meat

Who We Are

Fresh Meat Daily is a local meat business committed to providing the freshest, highest-quality meat to families and businesses in Nairobi. Operating from Dagoreti Slaughter House, we have built a reputation for reliability, freshness, and trust.

Our Mission

To provide fresh, hygienically prepared meat to our local community while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service. We believe that great food starts with great ingredients.

Why Dagoreti?

Dagoreti Slaughter House is a well-established, regulated facility in Nairobi. By operating from this location, we ensure that all our meat meets strict hygiene and quality standards set by local authorities.
