Who We Are - DM2 Chickens Kenya

DM2 Chickens Kenya is a premium fresh meat supplier based in Nairobi, Kenya. We specialize in providing the freshest, highest-quality chicken, beef, and goat meat to families, restaurants, and businesses throughout Nairobi. Operating from the regulated Dagoreti Slaughter House facility, we have established a strong reputation for reliability, freshness, exceptional hygiene standards, and outstanding customer service in the Kenyan meat industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver fresh, hygienically prepared premium chicken and meat products to our community while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service. We believe that exceptional food begins with exceptional ingredients, sourced responsibly and prepared with care.

Why Choose Dagoreti Slaughter House?

Dagoreti Slaughter House is a well-established, fully regulated meat processing facility located in Nairobi. By operating exclusively from this certified location, DM2 Chickens Kenya ensures that all our chicken, beef, and goat meat meets strict hygiene, safety, and quality standards set by Kenya's local food authorities. This commitment guarantees that every product you receive has been processed in a controlled, sanitary environment.
